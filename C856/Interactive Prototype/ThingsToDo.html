Things To Do In Taniti

Although our island is small, we're never short on fun activities to share with friends and family or to do on your own! Among these activities are:

  • Nightlife in Taniti City, from arcades to bars to sprawling nightclubs.
  • Visiting one of our many museums.
  • Ziplining through our beaitfully preserved rainforests.
  • Dive in our crystal blue waters and go snorkeling.
  • And for those who want to kick back and relax, a nine-hole golf course awaits!

Unfortunately, we can only list so many possible activities you can do here in Taniti, you'll just have to come and find out for yourself!
